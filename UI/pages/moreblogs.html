You’re invited to join cohort 30 of the Andela Technical Leadership Program; your ticket to a career in software engineering! Welcome to Andela. You are beginning an experience that will influence how you think, what you do, and what you will achieve for the rest of your career.


Because of your endeavor, you have been selected to be a beneficiary of the ATLP, a special program by Andela. After 9 months, you will become part of a 500+ Andela Global alumni who successfully passed through Andela and are now changing the world through cutting edge tech solutions they provide to their respective communities. Of course, we would like to inform you that there is hard work between now and your exit. Acquiring knowledge that will change you and your surroundings is not easy but we chose you from many because we believe our [you and us] combined efforts for your learning path will be a success based on our assessments. Meanwhile, we would like to bring to your attention some important programs that will take place for you to have a clear understanding of what Andela is and what is expected of you before we start our program.
